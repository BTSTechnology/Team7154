Drivetrain:
    2 Motors for each side
    Each side is controlled by a stick on the controller (forward and backward)
    Controller bumpers are for changing speed settings (RB for increase, LB for decrease)

Pnuematics:
    2 double solenoids for the pnuematic system
    Y-button is for the big piston (hold it down to fire the motor, release the stop it)
    B-button is for the small piston (same controls as the big piston)

Intake:
    1 Motor for the intake changing
    A-button is for activating the motor (hold it down to fire the motor, release the stop it)
